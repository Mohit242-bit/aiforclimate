# Climate AI Features Enhancement Plan

## 1. Carbon Emission Tracking & Prediction
- **Real-time CO2 monitoring** per zone
- **Carbon footprint prediction** based on traffic, industry, energy use
- **Net-zero pathway modeling** for each zone
- **Carbon credit calculation** for interventions

## 2. Renewable Energy Integration
- **Solar/wind potential mapping** across Delhi zones
- **Energy storage optimization** using AI
- **Grid stability prediction** with renewable integration
- **Smart grid load balancing**

## 3. Climate Risk Assessment
- **Extreme weather prediction** (heatwaves, floods)
- **Urban heat island modeling** with climate change
- **Flood risk mapping** with sea-level rise
- **Drought impact assessment**

## 4. Green Infrastructure AI
- **Optimal tree placement** using ML algorithms
- **Green roof effectiveness** prediction
- **Urban farming potential** assessment
- **Biodiversity impact** modeling

## 5. Climate Finance Optimization
- **Carbon tax impact** calculation
- **Green investment ROI** prediction
- **Climate resilience funding** optimization
- **ESG scoring** for interventions
